Stay a Night, Give a Night

Take someone off the streets with your stay in Seattle

Shelter provided by

In partnership with

Horizon's "Stay a Night, Give a Night" campaign allocates tourism dollars otherwise spent on expensive hotels or vacation rentals to help those who need it most: the homeless. Stay with a Seattleite during your visit to Seattle, and donate $14 per night as thanks for their generous hospitality. We've partnered with Mary's Place to ensure the funds are allocated appropriately. Mary's Place has a long track record of empowering homeless women, children and families to reclaim their lives.

For travelers, the there's a clear win: cost savings over a vacation rental or hotel, in addition to experiencing Seattle through the eyes of a local.

The Problem

Travelers: Cost of accommodations (hotel or vacation rentals) are a significant expense, and can prevent trips from happening at all.

Locals:: Homelessness is a growing problem. In Seattle alone, an estimated 1,200 kids sleep outside every night. It costs just $14 to provide a child with one night of food, shelter, and stability.

How It Works

  1. Complete our accommodation request form with details about your upcoming trip to Seattle and prepay $14 per night.
  2. We'll match you with a willing Seattle host. If we can't find a match for your host, we will refund your donation.
  3. Enjoy your visit to Seattle for a fraction of the cost of a hotel or vacation rental, and help the homeless in the process!

What is Horizon? We facilitate hospitality exchange with friends, friends of friends, and communities. We believe in giving back, and enable travelers to thank their hosts by making a donation to charity.
