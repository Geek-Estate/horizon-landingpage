How it Works?

Horizon facilitates hospitality exchange with friends, friends of friends, and communities. We believe in giving back, and enable travelers to thank their hosts by making a donation to charity.

The "Stay a Night, Give a Night" campaign aligns the giving with locals who need it most; the homeless. Travel is a privilege. We partner with local charity in each city to enable hosts to help fight homelessness by hosting travelers. What better way to give back than to provide shelter for someone without a home.

For travelers, the campaign provides a clear win. Cost savings over a vacation rental or hotel, in addition to building a strong relationship with a socially conscious local.
