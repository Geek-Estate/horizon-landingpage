UW Bothell

Global Alumni Hospitality Network

UW Bothell is community of over 12,000 alumni, within the broader University of Washington alumni community.

How do you tap into the UWB alumni community in travel scenarios? Horizon's UW Bothell Hospitality Network allows you to find other alumni based on location, with the main focus of exchanging hospitality in travel scenarios. Even if you aren't in need of a place to sleep, you can reach out for travel tips, or a meal/coffee/drink.

Why Use Horizon?

  • Search your Facebook friends, and UW alumni in one place
  • Control over which people & groups have ability to request a stay with you
  • Networking opportunities
  • Have amazing social experiences, with knowledgeable locals
  • Cultural exchange

Horizon's core mission is to increase access to travel opportunities worldwide by enabling shared hospitality among trusted contacts and communities; providing free or cheap accommodation and cultural experiences not otherwise available to them.

More in person experiences and friendships mean a stronger UW Bothell community for generations to come.
