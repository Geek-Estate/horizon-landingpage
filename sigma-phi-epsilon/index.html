Sigma Phi Epsilon

Global Hospitality Network

It's relatively straight forward to connect with fellow SigEp alumni who reside in your local area. When traveling or moving to a new city, the equation of how to unlock the power of the vast SigEp network becomes exponentially more complicated.

The way to strengthen any community is to facilitate more in person time among its members. The SigEp alumni community is no different.

Horizon offers a seamless way to find other trusted SigEps based on location, with the initial focus of enabling SigEp Millenials to provide hospitality to each other in travel scenarios. Even if you are not looking for a place to stay, the network can be used to find a fellow brother to have drinks or dinner with while on a business trip, a coffee appointment with a brother you end up hiring, or to find a SigEp to connect you with the right contact inside that company you've been trying to penetrate for a business development deal.

Benefits

  • Free and/or cheap places to stay with SigEp brothers across the country, and world
  • Unlock secrets from locals
  • Help SigEp brothers afford to explore the world
  • Amazing networking opportunities
  • Organize hundreds of thousands of alumni by location
  • The opportunity to say thanks by donating back to SigEp Headquarters to improve brotherhood for the next generation

Horizon's core mission is to increase access to travel opportunities worldwide by enabling shared hospitality among trusted contacts and communities; providing free or cheap accommodation and cultural experiences not otherwise available to them.

More in person experiences and friendships mean a stronger SigEp community for generations to come.

*Requirement: To access the SigEp network, you must be a member of the following FB group: Sigma Phi Epsilon Hospitality Network.
