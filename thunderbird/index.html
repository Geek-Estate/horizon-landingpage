Thunderbird School of Global Management

Global Hospitality Network

Thunderbird is the #1 ranked school of international business, with nearly 70 years of experience in developing leaders with the global mindset, business skills and social responsibility necessary to create real, sustainable value for their organizations, communities and the world. There are 40,000 alumni who work for more than 12,000 different organizations across 140 countries.

More in person experiences and friendships mean a stronger Thunderbird community for generations to come.
