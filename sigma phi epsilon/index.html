The Sigma Phi Epsilon Global Hospitality Network

Why

It's relatively straight forward to connect with fellow Sigep alumni who reside in your local area. When traveling or moving to a new city, the equation of how to unlock the power of the vast Sigep network becomes exponentially more complicated. The way to strengthen any community is to facilitate more in person time among its members. The Sigeps alumni community is no different. Horizon offers a seamless way to find other trusted Sigeps based on location, with the initial focus of enabling Sigep Millenials to provide hospitality to each other in travel scenarios. Even if you are not looking for a place to stay, the network can be used to find a fellow Sigep to have drinks or dinner with while on a business trip, a coffee appointment with someone you end up hiring, or to find a Sigep to connect you with the right contact inside that company you've been trying to penetrate for a business development deal.

Horizon's core mission is to increase access to travel opportunities worldwide by enabling shared hospitality among trusted contacts and communities; providing free or cheap accommodation and cultural experiences not otherwise available to them. More in person experiences that occur, the stronger Sigep community
