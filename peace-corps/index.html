Peace Corps

Global Hospitality Network

Peace Corps is a worldwide community of over 215,000 former and current volunteers to promote world peace and friendship.

As either a current volunteer or alumni, the chance to connect with other former or current volunteers in a foreign city you are either moving or travel to would be welcome -- yet unlocking the power of the Peace Corps network in travel scenarios is a bit of a mystery. Your only real option is to post a message in one of the many Facebook groups, and hope the right person happens to see it. Horizon offers a seamless way to find other trusted Peace Corps volunteers and alumni based on location, with the initial focus of enabling hospitality exchanges in travel scenarios.

Benefits

  • Free and/or cheap places to stay with Peace Corps alumni across the globe
  • Only see requests from those traveling to your city
  • Unlock secrets from locals
  • Have rich social experiences
  • Amazing networking opportunities with those who believe in the power of global friendship
  • The opportunity to say thanks by donating back to National Peace Corps Association

Horizon's core mission is to increase access to travel opportunities worldwide by enabling shared hospitality among trusted contacts and communities; providing free or cheap accommodation and cultural experiences not otherwise available to them.

More in person experiences and friendships mean a stronger Peace Corps community for generations to come.

**Requirement: To access the Peace Corps network within the app, you must be a member of the following Facebook group: Peace Corps Couchsurf.
