THE

PROBLEM

Prices are out of control.

There's no shortage of proof both rents and homes prices are on the rise. Many can't afford to live in our great city anymore as the influx of tech money continues to pour in. There's massive demand for short term rentals, and proposed legislation to cap it is the works. Building new housing units both expensive and time consuming, while 1/3 of existing bedrooms sit empty.

There are numerous organizations and programs trying to address the problem. Among them -- the Seattle Office of Housing, Sightline Institute, Zillow's Community Pillar, and Low Income Housing Institute.

Housing is a complicated issue, but there is one simple thing you can do.

OUR STORY

We help you find places to stay and live within trusted networks of friends, friends of friends, and communities.

It’s no secret, having friends in the city you’re visiting or moving to makes everything exponentially easier. Trusted friends, insider information & connections, are not just helpful; they are the lifeblood of amazing experiences.

Why? The world experienced face to face is remarkably better than the world experienced through a screen. This world needs empathy more than anything else, and lasting empathy only results from in person time, interaction, and deep understanding.

We KNOW access to community transforms lives – it’s our mission to make community accessible wherever you may be.
